Tierremar, Land of Eternal Grief is a Dark Elf faction found in Warlords Battlecry III: The Protectors of Etheria mod.
<!--more-->

This was last updated for TPE 0.9.1

### Changes

- Removes [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Annihilation (III)](#PAH2) from [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Orb of Sorrow](#BPOS)
- Adds [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Paragons of Loss](#PPOL) to [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Orb of Sorrow](#BPOS)
- Removes [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Annihilation (I)](#PAH0) from [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Orb of Sorrow](#BPOS)
- Adds [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Paragons of Grief](#PPOG) to [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Orb of Sorrow](#BPOS)
- Removes [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Annihilation (II)](#PAH1) from [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Orb of Sorrow](#BPOS)
- Adds [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Paragons of Anger](#PPOA) to [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Orb of Sorrow](#BPOS)
- Removes [<i class="xa xa-player"></i> Harpy](#AAHA) from [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Brood](#BAE1)
- Adds [<i class="xa xa-player"></i> Black Pegasus](#AABP) to [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Brood](#BAE1)
