Goblin Mountain is a Orc faction found in Warlords Battlecry III: The Protectors of Etheria mod.
<!--more-->

This was last updated for TPE 0.9.2b

### Changes

- Removes [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Blood Tribute](#PBLT) from [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Goblin Tower](#BAGT)
- Adds [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Reckless Charge](#PRKC) to [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Goblin Tower](#BAGT)
- Removes [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Tracking](#PTRK) from [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Goblin Tower](#BAGT)

- Removes [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Gruesome Shot](#PSGD) from [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Goblin Tower](#BAGT)
- Adds [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Ambushing](#PAMB) to [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Goblin Tower](#BAGT)
- Removes [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Blood Tribute](#PSCG) from [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Battleyard](#BABY)
- Adds [<i class="xa xa-aura"></i> Herbalism](#PHRB) to [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Battleyard](#BABY)
- Removes [<i class="xa xa-player"></i> Frost Dragon](#AADW) from [<i class="xa xa-tower"></i> Brood](#BAE2)
